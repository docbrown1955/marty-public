Actually applies the Fiertz identity.

This function applies the formula

\[ (\Gamma ^A)[\Gamma ^B] = \frac{1}{4}\sum _{C, D}\text{Tr}\{ \Gamma ^A\Gamma _C\Gamma ^B\Gamma _D\}(\Gamma ^D][\Gamma ^C), \]

with \( \Gamma ^A \in \{R, L, R\gamma ^\mu, L\gamma ^\mu, \sigma ^{\mu\nu} \}\)
and \( \Gamma _A \in \{R, L, L\gamma _\mu, R\gamma _\mu, \frac{1}{2} \sigma _{\mu\nu}\} \).

Parameters
initInitial QuadriLinear, should be standardized (see standardize()). To avoid unnecessary copies, this parameter is modified during the call.
posAPosition of the tensor \( \Gamma ^A \) to take in the first chain (default = 0).
posBPosition of the tensor \( \Gamma ^B \) to take in the second chain (default = 0).
Returns
The result in the form of a vector of QuadriLinear objects.
See also
https://arxiv.org/pdf/hep-ph/0412245.pdf
